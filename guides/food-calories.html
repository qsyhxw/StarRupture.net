Food & Calories (StarRupture)

If you’re constantly forced to stop and forage, you don’t have a “skill” problem—you have a Calories pipeline problem. This guide helps you stabilize early Calories and then upgrade to higher-value consumables.

Quick Answer

Eat Polifruit for an early Calories bump (11 Calories).

Upgrade Path

Use Recipe Station research to unlock Calorie Fluid → Calorie Gel.

Plan around Toxicity

Some high-value consumables add Toxicity. Keep a detox plan ready.

Beginner calories loop (simple, repeatable)

Rule: don’t “eat whatever” and hope it works—set a minimum carry. A good early target is to keep enough Calories to finish your current run and still get home safely.
  1. Carry Polifruit as your baseline (11 Calories each).
  2. Bank surplus food at base so your next run starts stocked, not desperate.
  3. As soon as you can, push toward Recipe Station unlocks for higher-value calories.

First upgrades: Calorie Fluid & Calorie Gel

These are popular “quality of life” unlocks once Recipe Station research becomes available. Since Early Access balance can shift, always confirm in-game requirements when you research.

Calorie Fluid (effect)

+20 Calories and +10 Toxicity. Stronger than basic foods, but don’t chain-consume if Toxicity is already high.

  • Research requirement (common): Star Tears + Polifruit (sources agree on Star Tears being 20; Polifruit count may vary by build/source, so check your UI)

Calorie Gel (effect)

+65 Calories. Big refill—ideal for longer runs where you don’t want to stop foraging.

  • Research requirement (commonly listed): 60 Polifruit + 25 Grubblers

Toxicity: keep a cleanup button

Purplant

Removes 40 Toxicity and removes positive effects. Use it after your buffs have done their job.

Practical rule

Don’t “solve calories” by stacking Toxicity. It’s better to eat slightly more basic food than to spiral into detox cycles.

FAQ

Should I rush Calorie Gel?

If your runs are constantly interrupted by eating, yes—Calorie Gel is a major convenience upgrade.

Why do high-value items come with Toxicity?

It’s a balancing lever. Treat Toxicity as a budget: spend it when it saves your run, then reset safely.

My research cost doesn’t match this page—what now?

Follow your in-game Recipe Station UI. Early Access changes can alter costs and even names.
